\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
intercepts Drop\+Events and pops up a Dialog to configure the source or the target of the object. 

Just drag and drop an attribute or a command from jive or from any other QTango panel into any of the QTango widgets exported by the Qt designer and quickly configure its source or target.
