A QGraphics\+Item (QGraphics\+Object) representing a circular plot. 

\doxyref{Qu\+Circular\+PlotI}{p.}{classQuCircularPlotI} is a QGraphics\+Item (actually, a QGraphics\+Object) draws curves on a circle, between a lower and an upper bound circumference
