z defines the z order of the element that is drawn 

\begin{DoxyReturn}{Returns}
the z order
\end{DoxyReturn}
Drawable elements with a lower z value are drawn before.

\begin{DoxyParagraph}{Important note}
Axes, curves, baseline and bounds are assumed to have z equal to 0. Drawables with z less than or {\itshape equal to} 0 will be drawn {\itshape before} the axes and the curves. Drawables with z greater than zero will be drawn {\itshape after} axes, bounds and curves. 
\end{DoxyParagraph}
